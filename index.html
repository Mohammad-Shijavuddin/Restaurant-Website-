About Our Restaurant

Adda the Restaurant in Rajahmundry is a popular dining destination known for its casual and trendy atmosphere, making it a great spot for both lunch and dinner. Offering a mix of small plates and all-you-can-eat options, it is ideal for groups and business meetings alike. With options for dine-in, takeaway, and delivery, it caters to various dining preferences.

Our chefs carefully select premium ingredients to deliver authentic flavors with every dish we serve.
